EVENTS

Technical & Non‑Technical

Technical

Idea Quest (Project Presentation):

Team

Participants present innovative projects or research ideas. Judged on originality, feasibility & impact.

Geek Quest (Technical Quiz):

Solo/Team

A fast-paced quiz testing participants’ knowledge in computing and engineering through multiple rounds.

Debug Duel (Debugging Competition):

Solo/Team

A timed contest where participants identify and fix bugs in provided code. It challenges coding skills, logical reasoning, and debugging speed.

UI Remix (Design Recreation Challenge):

Solo/Team

Participants recreate a given UI design from a provided source, focusing on pixel-perfect accuracy and tool proficiency. Judged on design fidelity and completeness.

Non‑Technical

Treasure Hunt (Crack Clues):

Solo/Team

Participants solve a series of creative clues and puzzles that lead to hidden treasures. This engaging activity tests problem-solving, teamwork, and observation skills.

Guess the Personality (Logo Finder):

Solo/Team

A fun quiz where participants identify famous personalities or brands based on logos or symbolic representations. It sharpens recognition and quick thinking.

Filmography
(Short Videos):

Solo/Team

Contestants answer questions or identify movies, actors, or directors based on film-related clues. This event celebrates cinema knowledge and trivia skills.

Photograph Content:

Solo/Team

Participants analyze and describe given photographs or create stories based on images. This event enhances observation, creativity, and descriptive abilities.
